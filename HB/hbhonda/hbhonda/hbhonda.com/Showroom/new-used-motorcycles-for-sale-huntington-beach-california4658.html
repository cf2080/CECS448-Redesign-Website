Motorsports Vehicles for Sale

New Motorcycles for Sale, Used Motorcycles & More

Shop our new & used motorcycles for sale online or come into the dealership. We’ve got all the top Honda ATVs for sale as well as the popular Honda Pioneer UTVs for sale as well!

Did you know we’re a Level Five Powerhouse Dealership? That means, if Honda makes it, we can sell it! Additionally, we will provide you with the highest level of service, genuine Honda parts, and repair available. Our factory-trained service department will keep your Honda motorsport running at peak performance all year long. We also offer online shopping for parts and accessories.

If you’re in need of financing, we offer a comprehensive online finance application so you won’t have to spend so much time at the dealership. If you’ve got good credit, bad credit or even no credit, let our finance professionals customize a loan package just for you. Don’t let a bad credit motorcycle loan scare you!

Huntington Beach Honda is located in Huntington Beach, California. We’re in Orange County serving Costa Mesa, Fountain Valley, Garden Grove, Long Beach, and all of Southern California!
