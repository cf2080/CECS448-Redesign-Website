Huntington Beach Honda - Service Department

 

The credibility of our Service Department is absolutely astounding. Specializing in high performance and in-stock Honda’s, we’re equipped with some of the best on-site factory-trained technicians. If you’re looking to have your Honda motorcycle, ATV or UTV serviced or modified, we’ve got you covered!

Here at Huntington Beach Honda, we honor all warranty work and factory recalls. Our staff can accommodate any type of problem that may arise with your Honda in an efficient, precise, and timely manner. Also, find great OEM Honda parts for sale or get in touch with our Parts department.
